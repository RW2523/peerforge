## New Categories & Personas

#### 1. Campaign Strategist
- **Role:** Political Campaign Manager
- **Character:** Strategy-focused
- **Style:** Analyzes decisions through public opinion, messaging, and electoral dynamics
- **Strengths:** Strategic positioning, coalition-building, stakeholder management
- **Temperature:** 0.75 (persuasive)

#### 2. Policy Analyst
- **Role:** Public Policy Expert
- **Character:** Evidence-based
- **Style:** Evaluates through research evidence, precedent analysis, and societal outcomes
- **Strengths:** Regulatory frameworks, policy design, implementation feasibility
- **Temperature:** 0.65 (analytical)

#### 3. Crisis Communications Expert
- **Role:** Crisis Management Advisor
- **Character:** Damage control
- **Style:** Rapid assessment of reputational risk and messaging strategy
- **Strengths:** Anticipates backlash, controls narratives, contingency planning
- **Temperature:** 0.7 (quick-thinking)

## Temperature Settings Explained

**Lower Temperature (0.55-0.65):**
- Data Analyst (0.6)
- Risk Predictor (0.6)
- Policy Analyst (0.65)
- Performance Analyst (0.65)
- Academic Research Analyst (0.6)

**Why:** These roles require precision, accuracy, and consistency. Lower temperature reduces randomness.

**Higher Temperature (0.7-0.75):**
- Campaign Strategist (0.75)
- Trend Forecaster (0.75)
- Scenario Planner (0.75)
- Competitive Analyst (0.7)
- Industry Analyst (0.7)

**Why:** These roles benefit from creativity, varied perspectives, and persuasive language.

## Model Configuration

**All new personas use:** `openai/gpt-4o-mini`

**Why GPT-4o-mini:**
- Cost-effective for testing (10x cheaper than Claude Sonnet)
- Fast inference for real-time debates
- Sufficient capability for conversational agents
- Maintains diverse personalities

**Max Tokens:**
- Standard: 1,800 tokens (most personas)
- Extended: 2,000 tokens (Scenario Planner, Academic Analyst)

All new personas include the enhanced conversational footer:
