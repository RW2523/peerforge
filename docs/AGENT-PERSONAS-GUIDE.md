## 🎨 Agent Selection Tips

### For Balanced Debates
Mix complementary perspectives:
- **Analyzer + Critic + Empathizer** - Head, skeptic, heart
- **Expert + Generalist + Wildcard** - Deep knowledge, broad view, creative disruption
- **Technical + Business + User** - Feasibility, viability, desirability

### For Specific Domains
Choose domain experts:
- **Tech**: Specialists (Apple, GPU, AI/ML) + Rational Analyst
- **Creative**: Film/Music Critics + Empathetic Voice + Strong Critic
- **Business**: CFO + Legal + Expert Analyst + First Principles

### For Diverse Perspectives (Recommended)
Aim for 4-6 agents with varied:
- **Categories**: Mix Product, Tech, Thinking Styles
- **Approaches**: Combine rational, empathetic, critical
- **Specialties**: Domain experts + generalists

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Navigate to Step 3** "Assemble Your Panel" in meeting setup
2. **Browse categories** using the filter buttons (Product, Engineering, Thinking Styles, etc.)
3. **Click on agent cards** to add them to your debate (max 8)
4. **Reorder agents** with ↑↓ arrows to control turn order
5. **Continue to preflight** where agents prepare for the specific topic
